About me

My name is Victor Meistermann, I'm a cognitics engineer.

After highschool, I spent 3 years of my life in a CPGE (a French post-secondary preparatory school for the entrance examinations to the French Grandes Ecoles) studying principally Mathematics and Physics.

After my examinations I entered an engineering school: ENSC - which can be translated as the Superior National School of Cognitics.

I discovered my vocation there. I have always been curious about everything and never found a field of studies that would match my multidisciplinary curiosity before.

In the end, the opportunity of studying the human mind and its functionning as well as the way a computer works among other things was an exciting adventure.

I knew that I wanted to leave France at one point, either to study or work. During my second year I was given the opportunity to spend my last year in California, on the campus of Irvine.
